Want to include your business?

Including your business is very easy so long as you can answer yes to the following important questions:

  • The business is independently owned, not a franchise nor subsidiary of a larger company
  • The business operates in a local community in Canada and/or is owned entirely by a Canadian citizens, permanent residents or those looking to become citizens or permanent residents. 
  • The business operates in a lawful manor, including respecting Federal, Provincial and Municipal COVID-19 Measures.
  • The business does not engage in illegal, exploitative or otherwise unethical behaviour locally or internationally.

Should you awnser yes to these questions then follow the blue link below and create an "Issue" on the GitHub page for the project listing the name and/or website of the buisness. 

Our team of volunteers will review your business and add your products along with some information regarding your business as soon as possible. 

Alternatively, you can contact one of our team members at matthew.giancola42@gmail.com and we will can start this process for you!
